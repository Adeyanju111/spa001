Overview

Austin Day Spa is a sanctuary of calm, blending organic elements with expert care. Our mission is to restore your balance through personalized wellness journeys.

Nestled in the vibrant east side of Austin at 2206 Hidalgo St, our spa offers a pristine, peaceful atmosphere. From the moment you step in, enjoy the aroma of essential oils, soothing music, and a dedicated team focused on your well-being. Open daily 9am–10pm to welcome you into pure relaxation.
